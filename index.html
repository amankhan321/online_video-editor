How it works

01

Drop a clip

Any video — MP4, MOV, WebM, MKV. It loads straight into the editor; nothing gets uploaded anywhere.

02

Rotate & frame

Turn a sideways clip upright, lock a ratio, drag the crop box to the action, or switch to fit-plus-blur so nothing gets cut. Thirds guides keep your subject honest.

03

Export with sound

Compatible mode encodes H.264 video and AAC audio with ffmpeg, so the clip — and its sound — plays in any app, not just a browser.
